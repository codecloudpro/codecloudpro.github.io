The problem teams feel every week

  • Selections get lost: emails, spreadsheets, and PDFs drift out of sync across teams.
  • No clean audit trail: approvals, comments, and change history are scattered.
  • Clients get confused: too many links, versions, and unclear next steps slow decisions.
  • Exports are manual: teams rebuild snapshots for every review cycle.
  • Risk goes unnoticed: critical changes slip in without clear visibility.

SpecSelect turns all of this into one guided, trackable flow with clarity for admins and clients.

How SpecSelect fixes it

  • Inventory-first: your products live in one structured catalog with variants.
  • Secure sharing: one-click client links with access control, expiry, and tracking.
  • Live selections: clients pick items in a guided portal with change tracking.
  • Revision certainty: version history with PDF/Excel exports on demand.
  • AI support: draft specs, flag risks, and summarize revisions instantly.

Flow overview

  1. Admin workspace: curate products, clients, and selections from the dashboard.
  2. Create a quote: add items, lock/unlock, and generate client-ready selections with AI nudges.
  3. Share securely: one-click links with expiry plus tokenized client portal access.
  4. Client edits & feedback: clients tweak, comment, and request changes with full history.
  5. Revisions & exports: snapshots to PDF/Excel with audit trails for approvals and procurement.
