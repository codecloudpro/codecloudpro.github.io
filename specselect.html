The problem teams feel every week

  • Selections get lost: emails, spreadsheets, and PDFs drift out of sync.
  • No clean audit trail: approvals, comments, and change history are scattered.
  • Clients get confused: too many links, versions, and unclear next steps.
  • Exports are manual: teams rebuild snapshots for every review cycle.
  • Risk goes unnoticed: critical changes slip in without clear visibility.

How SpecSelect fixes it

  • Inventory-first: your products live in one structured catalog.
  • Secure sharing: one-click client links with access control and tracking.
  • Live selections: clients pick items in a guided portal with change tracking.
  • Revision certainty: version history with PDF/Excel exports on demand.
  • AI support: draft specs, flag risks, and summarize revisions instantly.
