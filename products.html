SpecSelect Experience

From admin console to client checkout

SpecSelect lets your team curate, approve, and share selections with clients. Admins manage catalogs, quotes, and revisions; clients log in securely to review, refine, and approve.

Flow overview

  1. Admin workspace: manage products, clients, and selections from the dashboard.
  2. Quote creation: build selections, lock/unlock, and share secure links with expiry.
  3. Client portal: clients log in with a secure token to view, comment, and approve.
  4. Revision history: capture snapshots, track changes, and export PDFs/Excel.
  5. Checkout-ready: clients review, confirm, or request edits before finalization.

Admin dashboard: track clients, quotes, and AI productivity tips.

Selections overview: statuses, quick actions, history, and exports.

Review workflow: approve, request edits, add notes, export snapshots.

Share securely: generate time-bound client links with one click.

Client portal login: secure token-based access.

Client status: pending review notice and next steps.

Client cart: review, adjust, and submit selections.

Product details: variants, description, and edit controls.

Revision history: compare versions, preview, and export.
