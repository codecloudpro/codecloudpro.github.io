SpecSelect Experience

From admin console to client checkout

SpecSelect lets your team curate, approve, and share selections with clients. Admins manage catalogs, quotes, and revisions; clients log in securely to review, refine, and approve.

Flow overview

  1. Admin workspace: manage products, clients, and selections from the dashboard.
  2. Quote creation: build selections, lock/unlock, and share secure links with expiry.
  3. Client portal: clients log in with a secure token to view, comment, and approve.
  4. Revision history: capture snapshots, track changes, and export PDFs/Excel.
  5. Checkout-ready: clients review, confirm, or request edits before finalization.

Recruit Experience

AI-first recruitment and talent operations

Recruit automates sourcing, scoring, and approvals. AI generates JDs, ranks candidates, and keeps your pipeline organized from application to hire.

Flow overview

  1. Secure entry: role-based login for recruiters, hiring managers, and admins.
  2. JD & launch: AI-generated job descriptions, multi-department launch, public apply links.
  3. Pipeline & scoring: AI matches candidates to roles, ranks them, and tracks stages.
  4. Interviews & feedback: schedule interviews, record scores, and view score history.
  5. Conversion: move successful candidates to employee records with a click.

AI assists with JD drafts, resume parsing, candidate scoring, and pipeline focus so teams spend time on decisions—not data entry.
